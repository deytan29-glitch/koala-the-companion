Daily Check-In
Tap Check In each day to log your screen time. You'll rate how your day went and optionally write a quick reflection. Your koala rewards you with coins and XP for checking in honestly. Miss a day and your koala's health starts to drop — consistency is everything.
Today's Goal
Set your daily screen time limit using the Edit Goal button. When you check in, the app measures whether you stayed under it. Win the day and your koala gets healthier and happier. Go over and health takes a hit — but every new morning is a fresh start with no penalty carried forward forever.
Focus Mode
Commit to a distraction-free session lasting 10 to 60 minutes. Use the dial to pick your duration — every minute earns 1 XP, and a full 60 minutes gives you a bonus 100 XP total. For sessions under 60 minutes, tap the "+10 min for +20 XP bonus" hint to extend your session. Those extra 10 minutes are worth double the XP of a normal 10-minute block, so it always pays to push a little further.
Panic Mode
Feeling the urge to scroll? Tap Panic Mode to open a calming breathing exercise. Follow the pulsing ring — inhale as it expands, exhale as it contracts, in a slow 4-second rhythm. Use it any time you feel pulled toward your phone. Your koala stays with you the whole time. Tap the X when you feel ready to continue.
Coins
Coins are the in-app currency. Earn them by completing daily check-ins, levelling up, and finishing focus sessions. Spend coins in the Shop to decorate your koala's room with rugs, curtains, lamps, plants, a fireplace, and more. The cozier the room, the happier the koala. Coins do not expire, so save up for the items you really want.
XP & Levels
XP (Experience Points) is earned through check-ins, maintaining streaks, and completing focus sessions. Your total XP is shown in the top bar as a star icon. As XP accumulates your level increases — each level costs progressively more XP to reach. Levelling up rewards you with bonus coins and eventually unlocks new room themes. Your level is a permanent reflection of how consistent you've been.
Health & Energy
Health represents your koala's overall wellbeing. It drops when you miss check-ins or exceed your screen time goal, and recovers when you win days. If health falls too low your koala shows band-aids and moves more slowly. Energy reflects how active and rested your koala feels — it rises with focus sessions and check-ins, and drains during long inactivity. Keep both bars high by building a daily rhythm.
Mood
Your koala's mood is shown below the health and energy bars. It shifts throughout the day based on your habits, energy level, recent interactions, and whether you've been winning or losing days. A happy koala bounces and plays; a bored koala might wander or yawn; a sad koala slows down. Keep interacting, checking in, and doing focus sessions to keep the mood positive.
Streaks
A streak is the number of consecutive days you've met your screen time goal. Reaching streak milestones at 3, 7, 14, and 30 days earns big XP bonuses. A long streak also makes your koala progressively more animated and cheerful. Miss a single day and your streak resets to zero — but streaks can always be rebuilt. Think of every morning as a new opportunity.
Your Koala & Bond
Your koala reacts to everything — tap it to get a wiggle, wave, or surprised look. Over time your Bond score grows, unlocking more expressive behaviors and animations. You can rename your koala any time by tapping the name at the top of the screen. The koala's appearance and behavior reflect the time of day, the weather, your current mood, and how the day's going — it's always in sync with your journey.
Shop & Koala's Room
Visit the Shop tab to spend coins on items for your koala's room. Rugs, curtains, lamps, plants, bookshelves, a fireplace, and more are available — each one changes how the room looks and feels. Some items are locked until you reach a higher level. Focus Rewards are a special category of upgrades tied directly to completing focus milestones. The more you invest in the room, the cozier and more alive it becomes.
Eucalyptus Leaves
Eucalyptus Leaves are a special currency that fuels your koala directly. You can buy them in the Shop for just 2 coins each, and you can hold up to 15 leaf sets at a time — once you're maxed out, you'll need to use some before you can buy more. Your koala needs to be fed at least twice a day to stay happy and energetic. Keep an eye on your leaf stock and stock up regularly!
Feeding Your Koala
When your koala's Energy drops below 100%, a green "Feed Koala" button appears between the stat bars and Today's Goal. Tap it to open the feeding panel — then drag a leaf upward toward your koala. Once the leaf reaches the koala zone (top half of the screen), it flies in and restores about 28 energy. It takes 3–4 leaves to fill your koala from empty to full. When fully fed, your koala celebrates and becomes super happy! Each feeding also builds your Bond slightly. Your koala will start saying things like "I'm hungry!" and "Feed me!" when energy runs low, so listen for those cues.
Hunger Notifications
Your koala gets hungry twice a day — in the morning (around 8–10am) and in the afternoon (around 2–4pm). If your koala's Energy is below 50% at those times, you'll receive a push notification reminding you to feed them. If your leaf stock is empty, the notification will also remind you to buy more from the Shop. Make sure notifications are enabled so you never miss a hungry koala moment!
Premium Features

Earn 1.5x coins and XP on every check-in and focus session. Unlock premium-only room items like the Gold Koala Bed and Crystal Lamp. And as a premium member, your streak pauses instead of breaking when you miss a day — your progress is protected. Cancel anytime through App Store Settings.
Adventure Mode
Send your koala on a 12-hour outdoor adventure! Tap the Adventure card in the Adventure hub to begin. While away, your koala walks through a scenic landscape, journals on rocks, does yoga, and explores. Tap the adventure banner or card to open the full-screen view and watch your koala in real time. When the adventure ends, your koala returns home with coins and XP based on how long it explored. You can only run one adventure at a time.
Mini Games
Two mini-games are available in the Adventure hub: Table Tennis and Basketball. Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard before each game — harder difficulties offer bigger coin and XP rewards. In Table Tennis, drag your finger left and right to move your paddle and keep the ball in play against the Bot. In Basketball, swipe upward on the ball to shoot it toward the hoop — the power and angle depend on how far and in which direction you swipe. Each game lasts 30 seconds. Tap the X button in the top-right corner to exit at any time.
